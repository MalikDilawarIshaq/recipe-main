How It Works

Process Is Simple & Fast

The process is simple and fast. All you have to do is register to our website and start posting your favorite recipes for the world. Share the content with your friends and colleagues and get paid a share of all the earnings from your recipe.

Register To RecipeFund

In order to be recognised in the world for the appetising delicious recipes and be able to share your love for food with food lovers, you surely need to be a part of our community, so register now.

Start Posting Recipe

Share a step by step recipe of your creation followed by its process, upload the pictures of your finish product and be acknowledged by our community of epicures.

Earn a Share of Profit

Earn a percentage of profit from what you dispense. (Although we aren’t able to provide people with payment option as it’s a startup for now.)
